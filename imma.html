Irish Museum of Modern Art (IMMA)

I visited the Irish Museum of Modern Art three times during my trip. I kept arriving an hour or so before close, so it wasn't until the third visit that I got to see all of the musuem. There's lots to explore, with 3 galleries, an outdoor garden, two cafes, and a gift shop.
